## Welcome to my digital corner.

This blog is a collection of things that catch my attention. It’s a mix of professional experiments, personal ideas, and the "notes-to-self" that I think might be useful to someone else out there.

### What you’ll find here:

* **Cloud & Infrastructure:** Observations and lessons learned while building and breaking things in the cloud.
* **System Tinkering:** Deep dives into Linux configurations, driver troubleshooting, and making hardware behave.
* **Automation:** Scripts, workflows, and tools that save time (or were just fun to build).
* **Random Ideas:** My thoughts on technology trends, productivity, or interesting problems I've stumbled upon.

### Why this blog?

I believe that the best way to understand a complex topic is to try and explain it to someone else. This site serves as my public notebook—a place to document solutions to tricky technical hurdles and to explore new concepts in real-time.
